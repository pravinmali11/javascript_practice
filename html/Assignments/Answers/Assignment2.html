By Raj Khodase | August 29, 2024

The website https://picsum.photos/ provides random images for use in projects, often for prototyping or placeholder purposes. The images do not come with descriptions since they are dynamically generated from a collection of photos. Each image is randomly chosen from a diverse set of photographs and can range from nature scenes, urban landscapes, abstract art, animals, and more. The service allows users to specify the dimensions of the image in the URL to fit different design needs.
